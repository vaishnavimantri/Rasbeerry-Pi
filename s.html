Raspberry Pi 24

The Raspberry Pi 4 was launched on the 1st September 2009.

    The specs include:
  • Broadcom BCM2711
  • Quad core Cortex-A72 (ARM v8) 64-bit SoC @ 1.5GHz 1GB, 2GB or 4GB LPDDR4-3200 SDRAM (depending on model) 2.4 GHz and 5.0 GHz IEEE 802.11ac wireless
  • Bluetooth 5.0
  • BLE Gigabit Ethernet 2 USB 3.0 ports; 2 USB 2.0 ports. Raspberry Pi standard 40 pin GPIO header (fully backwards compatible with previous boards) 2 ? micro-HDMI ports (up to 4kp60 supported)

Here is a quote from RPI's website:

The spec and performance of the new Raspberry Pi 4 is a step up from earlier models. For the first time, we've built a complete desktop experience.
